Mollified irreducible multipole approach¶

Mollified irreducible multipole approach (MIMA) to Stokesian hydrodynamics

Mima3D¶

class pystokes.mima.Mima3D¶

Mollified irreducible multipole approach (MIMA) of a 3D Stokes flow MIMA resolves the fluid flow and solves Stokes equation directly. For further details of this method, read chapter 10 of the thesis: https://dspace.imsc.res.in/xmlui/handle/123456789/418 or at https://www.imsc.res.in/xmlui/handle/123456789/418
